Our story & approach

We bridge the gap between campus talent and industry needs. Our process is simple: recruit carefully, train with project-based learning, and deploy juniors into real product teams under mentorship.

Mission

To create career-ready junior developers and help startups scale affordably with trained talent.

How we work

  1. Recruit: Campus drives, coding challenges, and screening.
  2. Train: Short bootcamps focusing on web stacks, APIs, and testing.
  3. Build: Project delivery: MVPs, dashboards, and stable APIs.

Values

  • Practical learning
  • Mentorship
  • Transparency
